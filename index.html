We're a group of off-the-record, free, and unlicensed therapists, and mental health advocates!

Background

Founded on October 9th, 2020,, by Doctor Keefy, the original goal of PGWECN was to be a group where content creators could express themselves. However it slowly grew into a place for much more than that. A place for creativity, mental health, and of course, fun!
