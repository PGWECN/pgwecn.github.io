Welcome to PGWECN's Mental Health FAQ!

PGWECN is a group of off-the-record, and unlicensed therapists who look to help you however they can! If you're interested in learning more, we'll answer a few of your questions!

  • What is PGWECN's Therapy? - PGWECN offers venting channels, tickets, and a private team to help you discuss, deal, and cope with your real world struggles
  • What are PeaceKeepers - PeaceKeepers(or here on out known as PKs) handle our therapy business, and section of our server. They respond to vent, tickets, and help. They act as our off-the-record & unlicensed therapist
  • What do I do if I really need help? - We will ALWAYS recommend you to follow up towards a hotline, if you, or your loved ones, are in danger. You can find more about those here
  • Where do I receive PRIVATE help? - If you're having an experience where you need private help from one of our team members, you can find information, by clicking here
