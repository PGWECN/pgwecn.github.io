What are PGWECN Tickets?

PGWECN Tickets are a way for you to interact DIRECTLY w/PGWECN's PeaceKeeper team! Curious what a PK(PeaceKeeper) is, CLICK HERE, which allow for you to DIRECTLY to connect with our team in a positive environment in cases where you need to talk about your problems!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens in a ticket? - When you open a ticket, the PK team will be notified, and it can take up to 24 hours BEFORE a PK respond, in the case of emergencies, please call a hotline.
  • When do I open a ticket? - Anytime where you feel you need to directly connect with someone in a private, inclosed, and safe environment that can't be done publicly
  • What happens when I close a ticket? - When you close a ticket, you're immediately logging everything said in the ticket, and the conversations, and ending connection with our team
  • If I'm feeling really bad, where should I go? - We will ALWAYS recommend hotlines over ANY PGWECN Staff, so please try to contact one if your life(or someone else's) is in danger.
